About DUET

Dhaka University of Engineering & Technology (DUET) is one of the leading engineering universities in Bangladesh, committed to providing quality education in engineering and technology. Located in Gazipur, DUET has been nurturing engineering talent since its establishment.

Our institution focuses on creating skilled engineers who can contribute to the technological advancement of Bangladesh and the global community. With state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty, we offer various undergraduate and graduate programs in engineering disciplines.
